When it was all said and done, it was time to leave Philadelphia and head back to Laurel. Driving back from Philly, it was a little sad leaving. I really enjoyed Philadelphia. Chido's Review

{{date}}

With Neal asleep in the back seat and Jenn and I hungry, we both decided Mexican food sounded good. Once we arrived at Neal’s place, he suggested the same, so it was off to Chido’s, a Tex Mex restaurant next to our hotel (how convenient). We got there just before the kitchen closed (a common theme throughout the trip). I ordered the typical Margarita, while Jenn had a Virgin mystery flavored Mojito (I think it was Mango, maybe it was Melon). For food, I got creative and ordered a Taco Salad with Chicken. A tasty meal, but a bit too fulfilling for me. The chips and salsa provided was also enjoyable, though not legendary by any means.
