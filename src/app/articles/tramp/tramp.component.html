Still, the jokes were funny 'Orange is the New Black,' 'Putin on the Ritz' and everyone was laughing.

Afterwards, we headed up to the bar and plenty of gay-friendly music blasted, from Cher to Whitney Houston, so on and so forth. We complimented April on the wonderful performance and hung out for a few minutes. It seems April draws people towards her, so some of the people visiting looked even more out of place than typical. At least us Portlanders weren't the only one. Of course, Jenn and I were starving, and being in New York, we wanted Italian. So a trip to Little Italy was the perfect place to go for Italian food.

As Megan copied my drink earlier, I did the same and had the Chianti, but not with liver or fava beans, *cue the Hannibal Lecter reference *

Having been to Italy, I would argue it's much more different than here. After a night of Anti-Baby Farters and Creamy Cow Juice, it was late and Jenn and I were quite pooped.
