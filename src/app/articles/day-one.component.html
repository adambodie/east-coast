Baltimore

What a difference the time of year can make upon one's impression of the city. Having been in Baltimore for the first time during a snowstorm in 2013, the city had an entirely different feel during the late late spring evening when that snow and cold is replaced with clear skies and humidity.

Walking around the Inner Harbor was much more lively this time around. From people listening to a live band perform at the Hard Rock Cafe to the flock of people having a good time walking around, Baltimore was ready to party.
